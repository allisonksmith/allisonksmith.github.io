About

Hi, I’m Allison, a geographer, cartographer, and information visualization specialist. I make maps and graphics for Virginia Economic Development Partnership.

Before taking my current job, I spent a few months as an interpretive ranger in Shenandoah National Park. I’ve always had a love for maps but in Shenandoah I developed my passion for telling great stories. Every piece of data can tell a story if you let it. That’s where I come in.

Let's Talk Maps

This Land is Our Land

This infographic was created for a course in Graphic Design in Cartography where I was informed to create a longform infographic with a bivariate map. After spending some time working for the National Park Service, one thing that was evident to me was lack of funding to complete the NPS mission. The National Park Service is no exception to politics and Washington bureaucracy. This graphic was created to expose that.

  • Created: October 2016
  • Time to Create: 2 Weeks
  • Skills Used: Adobe Illustrator, IndieMapper
